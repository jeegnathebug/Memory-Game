Memory| Game

Instructions:

Click on two tile to be flipped. You can also press the key corresponding to the letter to flip tiles. If the two cards are a matching pair, the tiles are removed. If the cards do not match, they will be flipped over once again. Once all the tiles are removed and the image underneath is revealed, and the player wins.
